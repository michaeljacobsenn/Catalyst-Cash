02 / PORTFOLIO MASTERY

Build the Complete Financial Picture

The richer your portfolio data, the more precise your audit becomes. Add every asset class for surgical accuracy.

  • Checking & Savings — Connect all accounts via Plaid. Pro auto-syncs in the background so your audit is always working from current data.
  • Credit Cards — Track balances, limits, APRs, annual fees, and due dates. Catalyst identifies utilization compression opportunities across all your cards simultaneously.
  • Debts & Loans — Add every liability. The Debt Simulator uses the avalanche method (highest APR first) — mathematically proven to minimize total interest paid.
  • Investments — Sync brokerage, Roth IRA, 401(k) for real-time net worth. Pro keeps these current without manual intervention.
  • Crypto — Holdings with live market prices. Factors into your net worth automatically.
  • Other Assets — Real estate, vehicles, valuables. Classify as Liquid or Locked to let the AI reason correctly about your available capital.
✦ Pro Tip: Emergency Floor

Set your checking Emergency Floor higher than you think you need. The AI uses this as a hard lower bound when calculating your Safe-to-Spend. A well-calibrated floor turns your weekly audit from informational to truly actionable.

03 / AUDIT MASTERY

Getting Maximum Value from Your Audits

With 31 audits per month, you can run them far more strategically than weekly. Here's how to use them like a CFO.

1

Run After Every Major Variable Changes

Received a paycheck? Run an audit. Made a large payment? Run an audit. Don't wait for Sunday — audit when it matters.

2

Use the Dashboard as a Cockpit

Your Health Score, Safe-to-Spend, and Net Worth update after every audit. Watch your Health Score trend upward over weeks as a performance metric — the AI's analysis improves as your data set grows.

3

Chain Audits with AskAI for Depth

After reviewing your audit, immediately open AskAI: "Based on this week's results, what single action would have the most impact on my health score this month?" The reasoning model will think through the full picture.

4

Pre-Decision Audits

Before any major financial decision (large purchase, balance transfer, early loan payoff), update your balances and run a fresh audit. Let the AI model the downstream impact before you commit.

05 / POWER TOOLS

Mastering Your Pro Toolkit

📈 Debt Simulator — Avalanche Method

The simulator automatically orders your debts by highest APR first. This is the mathematically optimal approach — it minimizes total interest paid over time. Model extra payments to see exactly how many months you save and how many dollars you keep.

🏆 Credit Score Simulator

Estimate the impact of specific actions — paying down a card, opening a new account, closing an old one — before you act. Think of it as a pre-flight checklist for any credit decision.

🔥 FIRE Calculator

Calculates your path to financial independence and early retirement using your actual numbers, not national averages. Includes state tax modeling for precision. Update monthly as your net worth grows — watch your projected retirement date move closer in real time.

💼 Zero-Based Budgeting

Every dollar gets a job. Use the Unassigned Cash allocation method: assign every dollar above your Emergency Floor to a category until $0 is unassigned. This produces mathematically bulletproof monthly plans that your AI audit can validate.

📍 Geo Card Optimizer

Enable precise location. When you're physically at a merchant, the app ranks every card you own by its effective cash-back multiplier for that specific merchant category. Always swipe the right card. Over a year, this can add up to hundreds in extra rewards — automated, passive, yours.

📒 Transaction Ledger

Your full, searchable transaction history with on-device Edge AI categorization. Everything is classified locally using embeddings — no transaction data ever leaves your device for categorization. Search by merchant, filter by category, and export to CSV for external analysis.

Advanced Strategy

The Weekly CFO Pass

After your weekly audit: review the Ledger for any uncategorized or misfiled transactions → fix them → re-run the audit. Your Health Score is only as accurate as your input data. Correct data produces surgical recommendations.

06 / BANK SYNC STRATEGY

Making the Most of 10-Bank Live Sync

With auto-background sync active on up to 10 Plaid-connected institutions, your balances are always current — meaning every audit you run is working from real data, not stale snapshots.

  • Connect all active accounts — Primary checking, savings, credit cards, and any investment accounts you want to net-worth track.
  • Let transactions flow — Plaid pulls transaction history on each sync. Combined with Edge AI categorization, your Ledger fills automatically.
  • Monitor sync health — Occasionally banks force re-authentication. If a sync fails, re-link from Settings → Plaid.
  • Manual override works too — For accounts that don't support Plaid (some credit unions, employer 401ks), add them manually. The audit factors them in equally.
