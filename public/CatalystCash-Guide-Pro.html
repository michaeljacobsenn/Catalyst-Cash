Weekly Loop

The three steps that make it click

1

Keep your numbers current

Update balances, bills, and debts before you run the audit. Plaid helps automate this — but manual entry works just as well.

2

Run the audit

Start with the week color and the top recommended action. Use Catalyst AI CFO for deeper analysis on harder weeks.

3

Follow up when stuck

AskAI handles tradeoffs and what-if questions. Renewals handles cost control. Ledger handles transaction cleanup.

Pro Tips

Getting the most out of Pro

1

Use Catalyst AI CFO for your main work

CFO (GPT-4.1) handles all audits and deeper AskAI chats. Switch to standard Catalyst AI to conserve your monthly CFO quota on simpler questions.

2

Clean the ledger before big decisions

If your transaction list has errors, fix them before a serious audit. The AI reads what you give it — better data leads to better guidance.

3

Review subscriptions monthly

Use the Renewals tab as a monthly savings pass. Auto-detected subscriptions and cancel/negotiate scripts make it fast.

FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need Plaid to use the app well?

No. Manual entry works for everything. Plaid keeps balances current automatically, which makes audits easier to trust — but it's always optional.

Which AI model should I use for audits?

Catalyst AI CFO (GPT-4.1) is the Pro default because it gives deeper reasoning on complex financial situations. Standard Catalyst AI is faster for quick daily questions and conserves your CFO quota.

Does Pro change the financial math?

No. The core audit logic, debt calculations, and budgeting math are identical on both plans. Pro changes limits, model depth, and history access.

Is this financial advice?

No. Catalyst Cash produces educational analysis and decision support, not licensed financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.
