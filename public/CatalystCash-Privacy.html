Catalyst Cash Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-05 · Last updated: 2026-03-05

Catalyst Cash is a personal financial audit app. You control the data you enter. This policy explains what data is processed, how it’s used, and your choices.

Data We Process

  • User‑entered financial data (balances, debts, renewals, notes) used to generate audit outputs.
  • App settings (preferences, provider/model selections, and optional personal rules).
  • Usage metadata (e.g., audit count) if you use a paid API tier.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To generate audits and insights based on the data you input.
  • To render history, charts, and summaries locally on your device.
  • To enforce paid usage limits if you subscribe to a paid plan.

Third‑Party AI Providers & Zero-Logging Guarantee

🔥 ZERO LOGGING & ZERO AI TRAINING 🔥
Catalyst Cash ships with a secure backend proxy. Your scrubbed prompt is sent from your device to the Catalyst Cash proxy, then forwarded to the selected model provider. We do not store your raw financial payload or chat content on our servers. Sensitive names are scrubbed on-device before transmission, and upstream API usage remains governed by provider terms that prohibit training on API payloads. For paid subscriptions, the app may also send a RevenueCat app user identifier to verify Pro access without exposing your financial data.

We do not run a data warehouse for your finances. You hold the data, and the shipped app does not require your own API key.

Encrypted Sync & Backups

Optional cloud sync (iCloud) and manual backups are secured using AES‑256‑GCM encryption. If you set an app passcode, it is used as the encryption key. We cannot decrypt your data; only you hold the key. Manual backups exclude passcodes, API keys, device identifiers, and subscription state.

Local Storage

Your audits, settings, and rules are stored locally on your device. If you enable device backup (iCloud), Apple may sync app data according to your device settings.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not share data with third parties except to fulfill an AI audit or chat request through the selected model provider.

Security

All sensitive data including tokens, passcodes, and optional developer credentials are stored securely on your device. We use standard system encryption (Keychain/Preferences) to ensure your credentials remain private. Manual backups also exclude passcodes, API keys, device identifiers, and subscription metadata.

Your Choices

  • Use manual account entry without linking external institutions.
  • Clear history or factory reset at any time.
  • Choose when to run AI-powered audits or chats.

Plaid Integration & Bank Data

We use Plaid Inc. ("Plaid") to connect your Catalyst Cash app with your bank accounts (if you opt in). When you connect a bank account, Plaid securely fetches read-only balances and transaction data to assist with budget tracking and net worth calculation. We do not use Plaid to move money, initiate transfers, or make payments. Plaid's connection is read-only.

  • Data Minimization: Catalyst Cash only fetches the minimum data required (account names, balances, recent transactions).
  • No Server Caching: We do not store your bank data on any centralized servers. Balances are fetched on-device and kept strictly within your local App local storage.
  • Plaid Privacy Policy: By using the Plaid integration, you also agree to the Plaid End User Privacy Policy.

Information Security Policy

Catalyst Cash implements an operational Information Security Program (ISP) to protect your data:

  • Encryption at Rest: All application data, including API keys and cached financial snapshots, are encrypted at rest using iOS/Android device-level secure encryption (AES-256).
  • Encryption in Transit: All communications between the app and third-party APIs (including Plaid and AI Providers) utilize TLS 1.2+ encryption.
  • Access Control: Roles and access limitations are enforced via Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Only the verified device owner (via FaceID/Passcode) can access or decrypt the local storage constraints.
  • Vulnerability Management: We actively scan for vulnerabilities during development, patch identified security flaws within a reasonable timeframe, and monitor dependencies for End-of-Life (EOL) statuses.

Data Retention and Deletion Policy

Because Catalyst Cash is a "local-first" application without centralized database storage, you have total control over the retention and deletion of your data.

  • Data Retention: Data is retained locally on your device indefinitely until you choose to delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Right to Deletion: You may instantly permanently delete all local application data, connections, and histories via the "Delete All Data" button located in the App Settings > Backup & Data tab.
  • Plaid Disconnection: Disconnecting an institution via the App Settings immediately revokes our access token with Plaid, halting any further data flow from that institution.
