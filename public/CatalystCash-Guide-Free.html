01 / OVERVIEW

What is Catalyst Cash?

Catalyst Cash is a privacy-first AI finance app powered exclusively by OpenAI. It gives you the kind of advice usually reserved for people with financial advisors — instant, automatic, and entirely on your iPhone.

All AI requests travel through our secure backend proxy with PII scrubbing before reaching OpenAI. Your core financial data stays on your device. Raw payloads are never retained on our servers.

02 / YOUR PORTFOLIO

Everything You Can Track

The more Catalyst knows, the smarter its advice gets.

  • Checking & Savings — Connect via Plaid or enter manually. Catalyst monitors your runway and cash safety floor.
  • Credit Cards — Track balances, limits, APRs, annual fees, and due dates. Get alerted on utilization spikes.
  • Debts & Loans — Auto loans, student loans, mortgages, personal debt. The built-in Debt Simulator shows exact payoff fastest paths.
  • Investments — Sync brokerage, Roth IRA, 401(k) via Plaid — or add manually — for your complete net worth.
  • Crypto — Add holdings with live prices pulled automatically.
  • Other Assets — Real estate, vehicles, valuables. Classify each as Liquid or Locked.
💡 Safety Floor

Set a minimum Emergency Floor for your checking account. If upcoming bills would push you below it, Catalyst warns you immediately — before it's too late.

04 / WEEKLY ROUTINE

Your 5-Minute Money Ritual

Spend 5 minutes once a week. That's it. Here's the routine that makes Catalyst work best.

1

Sync Your Accounts

Tap Sync if you use Plaid to refresh balances. Otherwise, update your checking amount manually. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Run Your Audit

Tap the Audit button. OpenAI analyzes your cash flow, upcoming bills, debt load, and spending pace — then hands you a priority-ranked action plan.

3

Check Your Dashboard

Your Health Score, Safe-to-Spend, and Net Worth all update. Green means you have room. Red means act now.

4

Ask Follow-Up Questions

Use AskAI for anything specific: "Should I pay off my Visa or car loan first?" Get instant, personalized answers (up to 15/day on Free).

🔐 Privacy

All AI chat conversations auto-delete after 24 hours. Your financial data is scrubbed from prompts before any processing occurs on OpenAI's infrastructure.
