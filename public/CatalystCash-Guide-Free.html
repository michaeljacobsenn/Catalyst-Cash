FAQ

Quick answers

Do I need Plaid to use the app?

No. Manual entry works for everything. Plaid just keeps balances and transactions current without typing them in.

Does Pro change how the math works?

No. The core audit logic, debt calculations, and budget math are the same on both plans. Pro changes limits, model depth, and history depth.

Is this financial advice?

No. Catalyst Cash produces educational analysis and decision support, not licensed financial, tax, legal, or investment advice.

Where does my data live?

Your core data (balances, debts, renewals) stays on your device first. AI requests are scrubbed and sent through the Catalyst backend only when you use an AI feature.

Catalyst Cash provides educational analysis only — not tax, legal, investment, or professional financial advice.
