Honest Tradeoffs

When a narrower specialist may fit better.

Catalyst is the stronger default. These are the edge cases where a specialist-first tool may win.

For strict daily budgeting

Choose YNAB only if category allocation is the center of your financial life and you want to operate there every day.

For household collaboration

Choose Monarch if ongoing partner planning matters more than weekly audit, debt prioritization, or deeper action guidance.

For human financial planning

Choose Origin if you want access to people, not just software-assisted decision support.

For subscription-only savings

Choose Rocket Money if recurring-bill cancellation is the only problem you want solved.
