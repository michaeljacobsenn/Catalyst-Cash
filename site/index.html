FAQ

The questions people ask once they realize the app is broader than a weekly score screen.

Is this mainly an audit app or a full money system?

The audit is the center of gravity, but the product now includes Ask AI, budget, rewards, cash flow, renewals, portfolio, ledger, and layered recovery in the same weekly loop.

Can it help me choose the right card before I pay?

Yes. The Rewards tab ranks the best card for a merchant or category, compares the lead over backup, and supports nearby merchant choices when several stores are close together.

Does the budget start completely blank?

No. Catalyst can seed starter lines from recurring bills and audit context without overwriting the budget you already built.

What happens if I change phones or reinstall?

You can recover through encrypted export, iCloud backup on secure iPhone devices, or Recovery Vault and continuity flows. The site now explains which path is best for most people and which options are advanced.
