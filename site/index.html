How It Works

Three steps to a better money week.

Manual entry is supported. Plaid is optional. The weekly operating loop stays the same either way.

01

Bring your numbers current

Update checking, savings, cards, renewals, and major balances manually or sync supported institutions through Plaid.

02

Run the audit

Native math evaluates cash pressure, due dates, balances, and debt order before the AI turns the result into readable next moves.

03

Act and re-check

Use AskAI, renewals, budgeting, and the transaction ledger to act on the decision and rerun when reality changes.

FAQ

The questions serious users ask first.

Do I need Plaid to use the app well?

No. Plaid is optional. Manual entry still supports the core audit, budgeting, debt, and planning workflow.

Does Pro change the financial math?

No. The core math stays the same. Pro changes usage limits, model depth, history depth, and premium tooling.

Is the output financial advice?

No. Catalyst Cash provides educational analysis and decision support, not licensed tax, legal, investment, or financial advice.

Where does my data live?

Your core financial data stays local-first on your device. AI requests are scrubbed and routed through the Catalyst backend. Your raw financial payload is not stored on our servers; a short scrubbed excerpt of the AI response is logged for service reliability and purged on a rolling basis.
