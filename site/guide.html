Getting Started

Set up the smallest honest version of your finances.

Do not wait for perfect data. Load the inputs that can change this week's decisions.

Cash that protects the week

Debt that can hurt you

Financial Logic

The signals behind the recommendations.

FLR

Checking floor first

The audit treats your emergency floor as a hard safety boundary. If the floor is dishonest, the guidance will be too.

APR

Debt drag gets priority

High-interest balances are expensive by default, so the system pushes them forward when that is the financially clean move.

RNW

Timing pressure matters

Recurring charges matter because of timing, not just total cost. A small bill at the wrong moment can still wreck the week.

TRN

Trends validate the system

History should help you see whether your actions improved liquidity, reduced risk, and made the next month easier to run.

Privacy

What stays local, what goes over the network.

The short version is local-first by default, networked only when you use networked features.

Stored locally first

Balances, debts, renewals, settings, and audit history are kept on your device as the primary record.

AI on demand

When you run an audit or AskAI chat, the request is scrubbed and sent through the Catalyst backend proxy.

Plaid is optional

If you connect through Plaid, it helps keep balances and transactions current. You can also stay fully manual.
